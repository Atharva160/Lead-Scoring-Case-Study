# Lead-Scoring-Case-Study

## Project Overview

This project builds a Lead Scoring Model for X Education using Logistic Regression.

The objective is to identify potential customers (Hot Leads) and help the sales team prioritize leads with higher conversion probability.

---

## Business Problem

X Education receives many leads from multiple channels, but only a small percentage convert into customers.

The company needs a data-driven system to score leads and improve sales efficiency.

## Machine Learning Model Used

* Logistic Regression

---

## Model Performance

* Accuracy: **89.36%**
* ROC AUC Score: **0.94**

---

## Key Insights

Top variables influencing conversion:

* Tags_Closed by Horizzon
* Tags_Lost to EINS
* Will revert after reading email
* SMS Sent
* Time spent on website

---

## Business Recommendations

* Lower threshold during aggressive sales campaigns
* Increase threshold when minimizing unnecessary calls
* Prioritize high lead score customers
